Weddings & Commitment Ceremonies

The ceremony part of your wedding day can and should be more than just the "check-the-box" social requirement that it has become for many people. By being intentional and honoring your truth as a couple, it can be a sacred moment that sets a loving and celebratory tone: both for the reception party and the lifetime you're preparing to spend together.

"Jennifer crafted a beautiful, heartfelt ceremony filled with laughs, tears, hope, and of course all the love I could ever imagine."

We would be honored to guide you and your partner through your commitment to each other. While we are not religiously affiliated, we are deeply spiritual people who hold equal respect for all practices. Both of us are well-versed in many religious & spiritual practices and love to design interfaith weddings. We are also passionate about leading ceremonies for people of all sexual & romantic orientations: heterosexual and homosexual, monogamous and non-monogamous.

In our work together, we'll use practical mindfulness tools and get to know you as a couple in order to design a ceremony that's highly personalized to the story of your relationship.
