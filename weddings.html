Weddings & Commitment Ceremonies

Hire an officiant who will help you set a strong and clear intention for your marital or non-marital commitment ceremony and focus the energy of the group to aid in its long-term manifestation.

We proudly offer licensed officiation for heterosexual, homosexual, pansexual and polyamorous ceremonies.

We honor the elements of religious marriage ceremonies that strengthen an understanding of conscious relationship construction, customized to the specific vibe of a couple or polycule. Instead of religion-specific dogmas or rules, our commitment ceremonies honor the principles that are universal across traditions, which are:

  1. public witness
  2. verbal commitment to...
    • active and daily consent to the relationship
    • personal and interpersonal growth
  3. acknowledgement of the differences between the two halves so that there can be a celebration of the new whole.

Basic ceremony package

"Jennifer crafted a beautiful, heartfelt ceremony filled with laughs, tears, hope, and of course all the love I could ever imagine."

At the very basics, our officiant services include all you'd need for a genuine and straightforward celebration.

  • Officiant's signature on marriage license

All of our ceremonies are unique and any of our services can be combined to create your own personal and singular expression.
